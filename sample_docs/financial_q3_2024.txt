HPE Q3 FY2024 Financial Results

QUARTERLY HIGHLIGHTS

- Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR): $1.74 billion, up 33% from the prior-year period and up 7% sequentially
- Free cash flow: $955 million, up $114 million from the prior-year period
- Diluted net earnings per share ("EPS"): $0.21, down 72% from the prior-year period and down 53% sequentially
- Non-GAAP diluted net EPS: $0.50, up 9% from the prior-year period and down 4% sequentially
- Revenue: $7.7 billion, up 3% from the prior-year period and up 4% sequentially
- Intelligent Edge revenue: $1.39 billion, up 9% from the prior-year period and up 7% sequentially
- Compute revenue: $3.28 billion, up 2% from the prior-year period and up 5% sequentially
- HPC & AI revenue: $918 million, up 15% from the prior-year period and up 10% sequentially
- Storage revenue: $1.18 billion, down 3% from the prior-year period and up 5% sequentially
- Financial Services revenue: $928 million, up 3% from the prior-year period and flat sequentially

HPE raised its FY24 non-GAAP diluted net EPS outlook to $2.17 to $2.25, up 9% to 13% from the prior year.

FUTURE OUTLOOK

For the fourth quarter of fiscal 2024, HPE expects:
- Revenue: $7.8 billion to $8.2 billion
- Non-GAAP diluted net EPS: $0.52 to $0.60
- GAAP diluted net EPS: $0.42 to $0.50

For fiscal 2024, HPE now expects:
- Revenue growth of 2% to 4% adjusted for currency
- Non-GAAP diluted net EPS of $2.17 to $2.25
- GAAP diluted net EPS of $1.40 to $1.48
- Free cash flow of $2.1 billion to $2.3 billion
